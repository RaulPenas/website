About me

For the students interested in the seminar in automatig proofs of binomial equalities, check out the website

I'm a PhD student at Universitat Zürich, under the supervision of Valentin Feray: see here our group.

Research interests: Hopf algebras and Hopf monoids; Combinatorics of graphs, permutation patterns, posets and polytopes; Root systems and Coxeter groups.

You can see my master thesis, and a work on the chromatic symmetric function developed during my masters.

My research projects as of 31st of August, 2019.

You can also see more photos of me: building an oven, in my masters cerimony, and hiking in Kyrgyzstan.
